The Voice of dreams


Even in the darkness, it doesn’t fall,
Dream, imagine, rise and soar tall.
Be the personality that shines so true,
One day the world will bow to you.
Fly between the horizon of the sky,
Touch it and aim even more high.
Dive into the sea of hard work deep,
Harvest the dreams that you dare to keep.
Never feel feared, be bold, persevere,
You’ll have to cross each hurdle and tier.
You are the best human source,
Unleash your potential, your unstoppable force.
Each step forward, a victory won,
Each rising dawn says, “You’ve just begun.”
First be the star that shines in yourself,
Glow only when it’s too dark for help.

Responsibility: The succes Mantra

Being responsible, the success mantra,
Cannot be denied by anyone.
It's the path to destiny,
Adhering to it is essential.
Responsibility lights the way,
Guiding the mind both night and day.
Truly, taking responsibility is taking duty,
It's about embracing each opportunity.
Every day brings new responsibility,
New opportunities, new chances, new challenges.
Like a lantern glowing in the dark,
It shows the way and ignites the spark.
With every step, we learn and grow,
And let the light within us show.
This is the light that guides us from birth onwards,
It teaches us to stand up tall,
To rise again when we should fall.
To keep the faith when times are tough,
And know that strength is more than enough.
So take each duty with a proud embrace,
And meet each challenge face to face.
For when you own your path and choice,
Your heart will find its truest voice.
And when the journey feels too long,
Responsibility will make you strong.
With courage as your trusted friend,
You'll walk with purpose to the end.
